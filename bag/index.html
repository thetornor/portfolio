WHITE SHIRT BAG
When I was creating this work, I was thinking about the overwhelm of fashion. Therefore, I want to make a product that is sustainable. And it ends up with this grocery bag. I start by cutting some parts of the shirt and it was not working so I decided to cut it into a line and tide it together like crochet bag. I try to tie it by itself and tied it with thread. And it ends up using thread, even though it's wasting time but it will give color to this bag. The colors symbolize of groceries, green from vegetables and pink from the meet. After I finish the creation process, I decided to use it as my grocery bag. It is useable, light weigh, and easy to carry around.
