Khon, Thai traditional art performance.The actors will be wearing masks during their performance and each character got their own mask present each character. Many layers of papier-mache are applied to the form of the mask according to each character's features. This paper is made of rice or a unique handmade paper made from the bark of a tree called "Khoi". Up to 20 layers of Khoi paper are glued ontothe form, then the surface is dried and smoothed Additional layers of papier-mache are added, and the mask is smoothed with water-based clay and natural resin.
The decoration is finished using paint, lacquer, gold leaf shell, and colored glass fragments. For the finest examples, semi-precious gems are used, and real ivory is used for tusks and fangs.This work I was inspired by Christo and Jeanne-Claude'sL' Arc de Triomphe, Wrapped. They borrow the space and create a gentle disturbances to that. Even many people try to stop them but they think that the real exhibition of an incredible energy of human is in that space.
